about

know us

Ivy Gaze, LLC is a privately held, certified women-owned small business located in Arlington, Texas. Ivy Gaze, LLC is devoted to establishing and providing assistance to individuals and organizations on your cleaning, janitorial, or landscaping needs. Our services are provided to both government and non-government clients.

mission

Our mission is to make the earth a cleaner and healthier place through our cleaning services and use of sustainable products; one home or business at a time.

vision

  • Promoting a safe and healthy home and business environment through sustainable cleaning services.
  • Providing our clients and employees the best possible service and care.
  • Protect the health and safety of the environment and earth.
