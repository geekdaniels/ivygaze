about

know us

Ivy Gaze, LLC is a privately held, certified women-owned small business located in Arlington, Texas. Ivy Gaze, LLC is devoted to establishing and providing assistance to individuals and organizations on your cleaning, janitorial, or landscaping needs. Our services are provided to both government and non-government clients.

mission

Our mission is to make the earth a cleaner and healthier place through our cleaning services and use of sustainable products; one home or business at a time.

vision

  • Promoting a safe and healthy home and business environment through sustainable cleaning services.
  • Providing our clients and employees the best possible service and care.
  • Protect the health and safety of the environment and earth.

what we do

Home Cleaning Services

We offer daily, weekly, or biweekly cleaning services for homes, apartments, and rental properties. Services we offer include:

  • Kitchen, bedroom, and living area cleaning
  • Cleaning and sanitizing restrooms
  • Sweeping, mopping, and vacuuming floors
  • Recycling
  • Trash removal

Landscape Maintenance Service

We offer landscape maintenance services which encourages grooming and sustaining the visual appeal and health of the established yard. We offer services such as:

  • Lawn care aeration soil amendment services tree
  • Shrub Services Commercial
  • Residential Service Mosquito Control Tick
  • Flea Control Grub Control & Prevention

Daytime & Nightly Janitorial Services

We offer daily, weekly, biweekly and contract services for Schools, Churches, medical facilities, office buildings, and more. The services we offer are:

  • Breakroom and kitchen cleaning
  • Cleaning and sanitizing restrooms
  • Sweeping, mopping, and vacuuming floors
  • Recycling
  • Trash removal

Floor Striping, Wax, and Buffing

As foot traffic flows and time passes by, a commercial floor may become sticky, dirty, and unsafe to use. If left unmaintained, a commercial floor can quickly become dangerous and, in some cases, requires a new floor to be installed. Let Ivy Gaze get the job done so you can continue enjoying your beautiful floors!

Carpet Cleaning

Our service uses a hot water extraction cleaning method, often known as "steam carpet cleaning". This method allows us to safely remove dirt while cleaning the carpet and remove spots and odors without leaving behind any residue.

Latrine Services

We take pride in serving both government and non-government institutions and contracts. In our product range, you will find bathrooms, lavatories, and field toilets. We offer robust and reliable latrines that are easy to transport and set up. We are also offering timely solutions for servicing and hand hygiene options, too.
